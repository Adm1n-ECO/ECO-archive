The exchange

The more you share, the more you receive. The more you give back.

Every story enriches what comes back to you. Every person who joins brings something you did not have. Nothing that enters disappears.

01
Start with yourself

You are the center. Always.

You create your network and name it. You are at the center — not the oldest person, not the patriarch. Nobody else sees it from your position. Nobody else can build it from where you stand.

02
Talk. Nothing gets lost.

Stories emerge the way they always have.

Each person in your network carries the name your family actually uses for them — not the dictionary word, the living one. Everything surfaces through conversation. Nothing is a form.

03
Your Eternal, made visible

The journeys that made you, on one globe.

Migration arcs connect where everyone was born to where they are now — each colored by the cultural origin it carries. For the first time, you see your family not as a list of names but as movement.

04
Your Current arrives

What arrives before the moment passes.

Some questions have a window. The story an elder holds. The memory of a place that no longer exists. The right question surfaces at the right moment — specific, unhurried, never assuming. When you answer, it stays.

05
Your Current departs

What you share becomes someone's Eternal.

The story you share right now becomes part of what a younger person carries forward. You are always both — receiving and transmitting. What moves through you in this moment gets held, for everyone in your network, and the ones who come after.

Who does what

Three roles. One rule: you are always in control.

Conductor
$5.99 / month · No auto-renewal

Every member of the network. You share what you know, respond to what arrives, and control what others can see about you.

Keeper
No added cost · Designated by the founder

The cultural teacher of the network. Annotates profiles, authors vocabulary, creates curriculum. Title is community-defined — Elder, Storyteller, Granthi, Sensei.

Moderator
No added cost · Nominated by peers

Handles disputed relationships and flagged content. A steward of accuracy — not a gatekeeper.

Data ownership

Everything shared belongs only to you.

Everything in your network came from your network. Ancestry built a business from your family tree. 23andMe auctioned 15 million people's genetic data in bankruptcy. Facebook mapped your family connections and sold them to advertisers. Other platforms made you the product. Here, you are not. You never will be.

No auto-renewal

Every renewal is a deliberate choice. We earn your trust every single period.

Freeze, don't delete

If you stop paying, your network waits — not disappears. The stories are still there when you come back. If you ever want it gone completely, that choice is yours alone — total deletion, on your terms, at any time.

No data monetization

Your stories, your vocabulary, your network — none of it generates revenue for us. You are not the product.

The ECO Manifesto

Other platforms showed you the Root.

They drew the trunk, the branch, the leaf — and the leaf was you. They called the structure your history. But a tree without its storms is just furniture.

A tree implies one direction.
Culture does not travel that way.

A word moves through a grandmother's kitchen into a child not paying attention — absorbed through repetition, through the smell of the food it accompanied, through the way her hands moved when she said it. The child grows up and uses the word without knowing where it came from. They became a conductor of something they never consciously learned.

The coworker who brought food during the hard year.

The teacher who saw something in a child nobody else noticed.

The neighbor who encouraged your mother to cook when your busy schedule kept you away — and kept the recipes alive for the generation after you. The friend who stayed when everyone else left. They are in your field even if they never appear on the branch.

Here there is no tree.
Only energy — Compounding, Communicating, Conjoining.

The distance between where you came from
and where you landed.

Roots, passed forward.

Your Eternal is what it was, and what it will be.

Your Current is this moment — flowing into you from the people in your network, and out from you into theirs, simultaneously. You are always both. What enters gets held. What you give is already arriving in someone else.

Everything in your network came from your network. It is not ours to hold, sell, or transfer. You are not the product. You never will be.

Most families do not fit in a tree.
This was built for all of them.

Different words. The same beat.
