## THE TREE AND WHAT IT LEAVES OUT (LOCKED — core brand metaphor)

Other platforms showed the route, the trunk, the branch, the leaf.
The leaf was you. The structure was called your history.
But a tree implies one direction. Culture does not travel that way.

A word moves through a grandmother's kitchen into a child who is
not paying attention — absorbed through repetition, the smell of
food, the way her hands moved. That same word moves through the
neighbor. Through the song on the radio. The child uses the word
without knowing where it came from. They became a conductor of
something they never consciously learned.

A tradition survives because someone who married into the family
fell in love with it and carried it more faithfully than anyone
born to it. A language is kept alive by a teacher, not the bloodline.
A tree has no place for the teacher. Energy does.

A tree without its storms is just furniture.
The storms your grandmother survived changed who she was.
That change is in you whether or not it made a record.

The pollinators — the neighbor, the teacher, the friend who stayed —
conducted the current even without blood connection.
The ones in the shade carry your Eternal into a world you will never see.

Here there is no tree.
Only energy — Compounding, Communicating, Conjoining.

Compounding: adds over time. Each generation inherits more
than the last left behind. The recipe survives and grows richer
because the neighbor kept asking for it. This is the Eternal.

Communicating: moves between people. Transmits through
generations. Conducts through anyone whose presence keeps
the current moving — whether they know they are carrying it
or not. This is the Current.

Conjoining: fuses. Not connecting — conjoining. What happens
when cultures meet through love, hardship, and the long work
of living together. The Anglo-Colombian tea table is not two
cultures side by side. It is something new that neither culture
produced alone. This is the network.

The three C's map to the ECO triangle: three nodes, three
directions, three actions of energy in a living field.

Moving in all directions simultaneously.
Conducted by anyone whose presence kept the current moving.

Your field of energy is wider than any tree could hold.

## INCLUSIVITY PRINCIPLE (LOCKED)

ECO is not for "overlooked groups." That framing implies some
groups were well-served by existing platforms and others were not —
and that the former had something to do with building products
that excluded the latter. Neither is true.

The tree model was built for one kind of family: Anglo-American,
documented records, single-language, linear inheritance.
It left out most families — in every culture.

"White" or "Caucasian" is not a monolithic experience:
- Italian-Jewish families in New Jersey
- Irish-Spanish families in California
- Russian-Portuguese families in New York
- Any family where the grandparents spoke one language, the
  parents code-switched between two, and the children speak a third
- Any family where the cultural vocabulary does not translate into English
- Any family where the people who carried the culture forward
  were not on the bloodline

ECO is for any family whose story is too complex, too multilingual,
too cross-cultural for a tree to hold. That is most families.
In every culture. On every continent.

Language that positions ECO as being "for minorities" or
"overlooked groups" is inaccurate and exclusionary.
The correct positioning: ECO is for everyone the tree left out.
And the tree left out almost everyone.
