By continuing you agree to our Terms and Privacy Policy.

⏱ ~20 seconds
Design principle — magic link only No password field at signup. Ever. Supabase magic link sent to their email. They click it, they're in. No "confirm password," no "forgot password" for life. Eliminates the #1 reason people abandon signups.
✓ Confirmed: each user manages their own account. No social login required for MVP — email magic link only.

Step 2 of 4 · Your household

Name your
household.

This is how your family will appear to connections — a household, not just a person.

Can be your family name, a nickname, or anything that feels right. You can change this later.

⏱ ~15 seconds
Why "household" not "account" The product is built around households as the core unit — not individual people, not accounts. Naming it immediately creates the right mental model. "The Bakshi household" on the globe is immediately more meaningful than "vikas@email.com."

Step 4 of 4 · You're on the map

You're on the globe.

Your household is now live on EternalCurrent. You're the only one here — for now. Add your family to see your tree come alive.

San Jose, CA · Just you, so far

⏱ The aha moment
This is the most important screen in the entire product A single glowing node on a dark globe. No connections yet. The emotional message is immediate: your family is out there, and this is where they'll be. The loneliness of a single node is more powerful than a full tree because it makes the invitation feel urgent.
No payment asked yet The user sees the globe for free. They explore it. They feel it. Then and only then do we ask them to build their household — which is when the payment conversation begins. This is the correct moment.
