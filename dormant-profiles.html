The four lifecycle states

Every profile on FindMyTree exists in one of four states. The state determines what's visible on the tree, who can edit it, and what the node looks like. Moderators manage transitions between states.

Design principle: The tree never has empty holes. Every household — regardless of state — remains visible as a node. Shadow profiles show a silhouette so connections understand someone is there, even if their identity is not yet accessible. This keeps the network's shape intact and prompts stewardship naturally.
