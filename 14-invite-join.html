✓ Invitees join free. Viewing is always free. Building their own household ($1.99/person) is optional and their choice — never forced.
The three join paths View only (free, permanent), Build household (pay, full participant), Do later (session ends, link saved). All three are valid outcomes. The product should never make someone feel like a second-class member for choosing view-only — some of the most important people in a family river will never pay. That's fine. Their presence on the tree has value regardless.

✓ Decision 23 locked. Anonymous silhouette always visible to connections from the moment entered. Name and relationship revealed only after invite is sent. This respects the privacy of people who haven’t been contacted yet while showing the true shape of the family to those already connected.
Database states needed The pending_profiles table needs a visibility_state column: private (state 1), invited (state 2), view_only (state 3). Active paid profiles live in the main profiles table. The tree rendering engine reads both tables and applies the correct visual treatment per state.
