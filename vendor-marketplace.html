How a vendor partnership works

1
ECO vets vendor
Quality audit, pricing review, contract negotiation. We set the standard.
→
2
User configures order
Selects members, segments, options. All inside ECO — no third-party account needed.
→
3
ECO sends brief
Structured data packet (names, photos, tree structure) sent to vendor API.
→
4
Vendor fulfills
Prints, ships, delivers. Tracking number returned to ECO dashboard.
→
5
Revenue share
Commission or referral fee. ECO never touches inventory or fulfillment.
