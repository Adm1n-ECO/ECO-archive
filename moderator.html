Moderator system

Moderators are trusted members of your network who volunteer to keep the tree healthy. The system identifies the top 3 most active users and invites them to step forward. No fixed threshold — the algorithm adapts to your tree's size.

Decision 31 — locked. The system nominates the top 3 most active users in your network. Activity is scored by logins, content additions, connections initiated, and events shared. No fixed threshold — a 5-person family tree and a 200-node network each get the right number of candidates automatically.
Activity scoring algorithm
1
Login frequency — weighted by recency. A login yesterday scores higher than one last month. Exponential decay over 90 days.
2
Content contributions — profile updates, photos uploaded, life events posted, household bio written.
3
Network engagement — connections initiated, invitations sent, merge requests reviewed.
4
Top 3 only — candidates ranked, top 3 surface. Existing moderators are excluded from the pool. Decided S2

Decision 30 — locked. Full moderator scope. All 5 powers below are available to any active moderator.
Power Who can use it Reversible? Description
Approve stewardship Moderator Yes Approve, reassign, or remove stewards from shadow and kept-alive profiles.
Dispute arbitration Moderator Yes Make a binding ruling on content disputes. Either party can appeal to the tree owner.
Mark In Memoriam Moderator + Direct conn. 7-day window Opens a 7-day window for acknowledgement before the status is finalised. Can be contested by any direct connection during the window.
Content removal Moderator Yes (30 days) Remove photos, bios, or events deemed inappropriate. Removed content is soft-deleted for 30 days before permanent erasure.
Nominate new moderators Moderator — Invite additional volunteers from the system-generated candidate list. Cannot exceed 3 active moderators per tree.
Remove a moderator Owner only Yes Only the tree owner can revoke moderator status. Prevents moderators from removing each other.
Moderators cannot: delete households, change subscription status, access billing information, view private-tier connection details, or act on profiles outside their own network segment.
